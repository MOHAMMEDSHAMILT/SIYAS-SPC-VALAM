How It Works

1

Deploy Regenerative Fertilizers

Farmers apply our specialized organic manure and plant food to improve soil health and sequester carbon naturally.

2

Satellite AI Monitoring

Boomitra's advanced satellite technology measures soil carbon levels from space, verifying sequestration without expensive soil testing.

3

Direct Payments

Verified credits are sold to corporations and governments, with the majority of revenue going directly to the farmers.

High-Integrity Credits

We believe in transparency. Our data is rigorously verified by third-party standards, ensuring that every credit represents a real, measurable reduction in atmospheric carbon. This isn't just about offsetting emissions; it's about investing in the future of food security and climate resilience.
