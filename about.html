Our Story

At SPC x Boomitra, we see ourselves not just as a company, but as a movement. We act as a "Friend of the Earth," bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and traditional farming wisdom.

Our mission is simple: empower small-holder farmers. We provide them with the essential tools—high-quality organic manure and precision plant food—to restore their soil's health. But we don't stop there. Through our partnership with Boomitra, we unlock financial incentives for these farmers by measuring the carbon they sequester.

We do not own the land—the farmers do.

Our role is to support, facilitate, and ensure that the guardians of our soil are rewarded for their vital work in combating climate change.
